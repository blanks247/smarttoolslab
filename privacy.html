Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 Welcome to SmartToolsLab. Your privacy is critically important to us. Our core philosophy is to build powerful tools that respect your privacy by running directly in your web browser. 1. Client-Side Processing Almost all tools on SmartToolsLab (including Image Compressor, PDF Merge, JSON Formatter, Word Counter, etc.) operate entirely on the client-side. This means processing happens directly within your web browser. Your files, texts, and data are NEVER uploaded to our servers. 2. Data Collection & Analytics We use basic web analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site (e.g., page views, time on site). This data is aggregated, anonymized, and does not identify you personally. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect personal email addresses or names unless you explicitly provide them via our contact form. 3. Advertising & Cookies We may use third-party advertising companies (such as Google AdSense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. 4. Third-Party Links Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., affiliate links). We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of any third-party sites or services. 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating this page. Your continued use of the website following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes. 6. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
