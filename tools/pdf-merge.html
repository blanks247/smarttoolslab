Technical Overview: How it Works

SmartToolsLab utilizes the powerful PDF-lib library. When you select your files, our application reads them as binary data within your browser's memory. The merging process involves creating a new PDF document and copying each page from your source files into the new one. This approach ensures that your original files remain untouched and your private information is never transmitted over the internet.

This method is not only more secure but also significantly more efficient for large files, as there is no network overhead for uploading and downloading.

Absolutely. Your safety is our priority. Since all processing happens in your browser, your files never leave your device. We don't have access to your data, and we don't store any copies on our servers.

Currently, we support merging two or more PDFs simultaneously. There is no hard-coded limit on the number of files, though very large numbers of files may be limited by your browser's available memory.

No, the quality remains exactly the same. Our tool copies the original pages directly, so there is no re-compression or loss of resolution in images or text.
