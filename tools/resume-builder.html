Expert Resume Writing Tips

  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with words like "Managed," "Developed," "Optimized," or "Achieved."
  • Quantify Results: Instead of saying "increased sales," say "increased sales by 20% over 6 months."
  • Keep it Concise: Aim for a one-page resume if you have less than 10 years of experience.
  • Customization: Tailor your professional summary and skills for every job application to match the job description.
