How to Count Words Effectively

When writing for different platforms, word counts vary significantly. For example, a standard SEO-optimized blog post should be at least 1,000 words, while a Twitter (X) post is limited by character count (280). Our tool helps you navigate these limits by providing a clear view of both metrics simultaneously.

Pro Tip: Most academic essays require a 1.5 or 2.0 line spacing, which equates to roughly 250-300 words per page. Use our counter to estimate your page length as you write.

Reading Time vs Speaking Time

Did you know the average adult reads at about 238 words per minute (WPM), but speaks at only 130-150 WPM? If you're preparing a speech or a video script, use our word counter to estimate how long your delivery will take.

  • 1 Minute Speech: ~130 words
  • 5 Minute Presentation: ~650 words
  • 10 Minute Keynote: ~1,300 words
